Our classes

Classes

At Sisep Education Institute, our dedicated teachers provide a nurturing environment for Grades 1 to 5. We offer a well-rounded curriculum covering Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies, along with enriching subjects like Art and Physical Education. Our focus is on building strong academic foundations and fostering a love of learning.

At Sisep Education Institute, our expert teachers guide students in Grades 6 to 11 through a comprehensive curriculum. Core subjects include Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies, with additional offerings in Art, Music, and Physical Education. We focus on critical thinking, creativity, and academic excellence.

At Sisep Education Institute, our experienced teachers provide specialized instruction for Grades 12 to 13 (A/L). We offer advanced courses in Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies, along with electives in Business, Computer Science, and Humanities. Our rigorous curriculum prepares students for higher education and career success.

Facilities

At Sisep Education Institute, our state-of-the-art IT, Physics, and Chemistry labs offer hands-on learning experiences. Our knowledgeable teachers guide students through practical applications of their coursework, enhancing understanding in subjects like programming, experimental physics, and chemical reactions. These facilities foster innovation and in-depth exploration.
