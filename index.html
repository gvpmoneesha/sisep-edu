Classes we offer

At Sisep Education Institute, we provide comprehensive educational programs for students from Grade 1 to Grade 12.
Our curriculum covers all subjects, ensuring a well-rounded education that prepares students for future academic success and personal growth.

Grade 1-5

At Sisep Education Institute, our Grades 1 to 5 program provides a comprehensive and engaging curriculum that covers all essential subjects. Students will explore Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies, building a strong academic foundation. Additionally, our curriculum includes Art, Music, and Physical Education, ensuring a well-rounded education. Our dedicated teachers create a supportive environment that fosters a love of learning and encourages curiosity and growth.

Grade 6-11

At Sisep Education Institute, our Grades 6 to 11 program offers a robust curriculum designed to challenge and inspire students. Core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies are complemented by elective courses in Art, Music, Technology, and Physical Education. Our experienced educators provide a stimulating learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and personal growth, preparing students for academic success and future endeavors.

Grade 12-13

At Sisep Education Institute, our Grades 12 to 13 program is designed to prepare students for higher education and future careers. We offer advanced courses in Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies, along with specialized subjects such as Business Studies, Computer Science, and Humanities. Our curriculum also includes enriching electives in Art, Music, and Physical Education. With a focus on critical thinking, problem-solving, and in-depth knowledge, our experienced educators guide students towards academic excellence and personal development.
